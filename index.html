WHAT IS BID WARS ?

Bidding Day

29th September 2020.


Bid Wars is a unique concept where your favorite streamer will be bidding for you! Yes, you heard that right. Once you register, you will be part of the talent pool which will be made available to the 8 captains who will be bidding for the most desired players to be a part of their team. Each captain will have a limited budget so they will have to spend their currency wisely in order to get the best talent! The selected players get to play alongside their captains in a high-stakes tournament where the winner takes it all!
